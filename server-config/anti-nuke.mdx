Subcommand: `antinuke bans` <br /> Module: `antinuke` <br /> Description: Prevent members from being banned.

<div id="bans"/>
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Example">
  `antinuke bans true --threshold 4 --duration 5m`
  <Frame caption="Enabling antinuke bans and setting flags">
  <img src="/images/security/4.gif" />
  </Frame>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Syntax">`antinuke bans <status> [...flags]`</Tab>
  <Tab title="Punishment Types">
    No flags for punishment are avaliable for this command.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
